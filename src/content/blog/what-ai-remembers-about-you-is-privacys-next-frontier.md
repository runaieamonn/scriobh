Good actionable advice in [this article by Bogen & Joshi][1] on how to make AI more privacy-respecting in its use of personal data for personalization:

“*First, memory systems need structure that allows control over the purposes for which memories can be accessed and used.
…
Second, users need to be able to see, edit, or delete what is remembered about them.
…
Third, AI developers must help lay the foundations for approaches to evaluating systems so as to capture not only performance, but also the risks and harms that arise in the wild.*”
